About

StritGRAD offers customized selling tools that market financial services through unique contact centre infrastructure and effective field processes.

Our tools and processes harvest practicable solutions to reach an even wider conservative audience that is still not technologically active and is marginalized from enjoying essential mainstream financial products such as insurance plans.

Our young firm boasts competent, well skilled and knowledgeable workforce capable of delivering premium sales and customer service solutions suitable for various industries including Telecommunications, Insurance, Pharmaceuticals, Banking and Retail.

Services

Contact centre and Home-based operations allow us to effectively deliver well-organized, bespoke and flexible telemarketing services ideal and convenient for any clientele seven days a week across multiple digital distribution channels. With a multilingual workforce on phones, our Field teams engage on ground activations in various identified areas nationwide focusing sales, lead generation and brand representation.

  • Field Sales
  • Outbound Calls
  • Leads Generation
  • Policy Maintenance
  • Courtesy and Collections

CSI initiative

For side-lined communities to develop and thrive, new opportunities must evolve to help alleviate poverty among targeted social challenges.

Our central value at StritGRAD is to give back to poor societies through collaborative efforts undertaken with our channel partners and stakeholders, and help facilitate work skills, educational and entrepreneurship initiatives for locals particularly the youth in order to achieve job creation outcomes.

  • Phutha Sechaba
  • Naledi Youth Initiative
  • Youth Entrepreneurial Exposition

Management Team

Joseph Khoza
Managing Director

Founded StritGRAD after serving in various brokers and BPO firms. He started his career in telecommunications with focus on long-term insurance where he first served as an agent, before managing in companies like Hollard, Esurity Financial Services, MI-Group, U-Covered and Corner Intermediaries.

His career accomplishments prior to starting StritGRAD group include building a contact centre structure for Corner Intermediaries to market their financial products through digital campaigns, preventing the closure of Esurity outbound call centre through production and quality enhancements.

More importantly, Joseph was part of the leadership team that introduced a remote contact centre project at Hollard working on recycled data.

Strategic channel partnerships, corporate administration and acumen for sales are part of his core strength and his capabilities have further improved and developed his skills over the years. A confident business leader who excels in building new and diverse markets.
